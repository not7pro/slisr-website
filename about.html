About Us

Discover our legacy of excellence and commitment to education

Our History

A legacy of excellence woven through decades

The Foundation

Founded on September 17, 1988, as the Sri Lankan Embassy International School. We began our journey with just 11 students and 4 teachers, established by expatriates under the patronage of the Sri Lankan Embassy.

Community Growth

A period of rapid enrollment growth saw the school evolve from embassy involvement to a community-elected Board of Management model, strengthening our roots in Riyadh.

Expansion & Modernization

We consolidated English-medium education based on British curriculum frameworks. From a single villa, we expanded to three buildings, inaugurating the girls' wing in 2007 and modernizing the primary section.

Academic Strengthening

Firmly established as a Pearson/Edexcel center, we opened new science, library, and computer labs to accommodate growing numbers and enhance our academic offerings.

Silver Jubilee

Celebrated 25 years of educational excellence with community leaders and the Sri Lankan Ambassador, marking a quarter-century of success.

Global Recognition

Our students consistently achieved top rankings in the country, region, and world in Pearson Edexcel examinations, cementing our reputation for high academic standards.

Resilience

Maintained full operations during the global pandemic, adapting with virtual learning and ensuring no disruption to examinations and student progress.

New Campus

A historic milestone: Moved into a purpose-built school campus, providing state-of-the-art facilities for our growing student body.

Alumni Association

Ambassador Ameer Ajwad officially launched the SLISR Alumni Association during his first visit, connecting generations of our graduates.

A Center of Excellence

Today, we stand as an independent, non-profit community school offering a complete British curriculum (Pre-KG to Grade 12) with over 80 staff and hundreds of successful students.

Our Mission

To nurture young minds through quality education rooted in Sri Lankan heritage, fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and character development that prepares students for global citizenship and lifelong success.

Our Vision

To be the premier international school in Saudi Arabia, recognized for academic excellence, cultural integration, and holistic development that empowers students to become confident, compassionate leaders of tomorrow.
